Our Story

Biskit and Miskit are not just cats — they are the heartbeat of my home where i have to stay whole day-night. Between coding sessions and coffee breaks, they are my chaos, my calm, and my constant inspiration. While sleeping, they sit by my side and sleep too which reduce my stress and give me peace.

My apartment feels alive with them. From late-night keyboard naps to early morning cuddles, every moment is a warm reminder that companionship does not always need words. Their silent presence brings balance and joy into my work-from-home life.
